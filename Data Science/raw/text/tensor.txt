--- page 8 ---
Sparse tensor
•
Compressed sparse row  (CSR)
Three arrays:
V: Non-zero values
COL_INDEX: Column index of the non-zero elements
ROW_INDEX (length m+1): encodes the index in V and COL_INDEX where the given 
row starts.
To reconstruct a row:
 
row_start = ROW_INDEX[row] 
 
row_end = ROW_INDEX[row + 1]
Make the slices 
V[row_start:row_end]  values
COL_INDEX[row_start:row_end] column indexes
