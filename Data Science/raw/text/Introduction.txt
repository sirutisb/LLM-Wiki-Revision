The data sources are changing
• Before 1980
Data was generated by machines
• From 1980 to 2000
Data was being generated by  employees resulting from processes within organisations
 
Payroll, bank transactions, sales
• 2000~2005
Data was generated by the end users
 
Videos, blog posts, photos
• From ~2005
Data also started to be generated by devices, hardware and applications
 
Mobile phones, remote sensors, logs

--- page 14 ---
Examples
• IoT includes mobile devices, microphones, readers/sensors/scanners that generate data. 
• CCTV for security and military surveillance, animal tracker, etc. 
• Call logs analytics in the customer service centre. 
• An aircraft can generate several terabytes of data per flight.
• Smart meters in power grids read the usage every 15 minutes and records 350 billion 
transactions in a year.
