--- page 2 ---
Online learning
• There are three types of data shifts that will cause model decay
• Covariate shifts
Changes in the input variables from the training data to the test data while the target variables are 
unchanged
•
Face recognition not being able to identify people wearing masks
• Prior probability shifts
Changes in the target variables while input variables are unchanged
•
A model that could estimate the probability of someone having flu based on their symptoms during the 
COVID pandemic
• Concept drifts
The relationship between input and output change over time in unforeseen ways

Concept drift
• Streaming data is dynamic and the 
underlying statistical characteristics can 
(and probably will) change over time
E.g. Consumer preferences and behaviours
• Gradual changes: cultural or economic 
changes
• Abrupt changes: Pandemic
• Recurring or  cyclical changes:  seasons
• Batch learner models will become 
outdated
Source: media.giphy.com

--- page 5 ---
ADWIN
• It maintains a window of variable size that 
adjusts itself in face of a drift
•
It keeps the most recent data points in the 
stream and discards the older ones
•
It grows the window when no change is 
apparent
•
Shrinks it when data changes
