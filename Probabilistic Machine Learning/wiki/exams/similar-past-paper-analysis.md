## Practical Takeaway

The existing revision plans were broadly accurate for COM3031 content, but several understated the old-paper habit of mixing a short conceptual part with a calculation/derivation part. The new practice additions therefore focus on exam phrasing and transfer drills rather than expanding the official scope.
