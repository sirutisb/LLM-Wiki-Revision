Summary
Variational inference (VI):
• Approximates the posterior with a chosen

• Turns inference into an optimisation

• Objective: minimise reverse KL

• Maximising ELBO ≡minimising KL

Mean-ﬁeld VI:
• Assumes independence under 𝑞:
• Enables block coordinate ascent updates
• Fast and scalable, Often analytic updates
Parametric VI:
• Parameterise 𝑞directly (e.g. Gaussian)
• Optimise ELBO using gradients
• More ﬂexible, widely used in modern ML
