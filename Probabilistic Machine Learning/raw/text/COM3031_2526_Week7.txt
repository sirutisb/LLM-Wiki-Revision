--- page 18 ---
Why Autoencoders Are Still 
Not Enough
18
Deterministic Latent Representation
• Each input maps to a single point.
• No notion of uncertainty.
• No probability model for z
No Explicit Structure in Latent Space
Even with DAE or sparsity:
• Nearby points in latent space may not 
correspond to meaningful variations.
• The latent space can be irregular or 
discontinuous.
• Sampling random z may produce meaningless 
outputs
Weak Generative Capability
• We learned good representations, but not a 
generative model.
