What is a Commodity Cluster?
A cluster architecture where both the compute nodes and the interconnect are commercial off-the-shelf (OTS) products, providing a cost-effective balance of performance.

What are GPU "Warps"?
Groups of 32 threads in an NVIDIA GPU that execute the same instruction in lockstep (SIMD execution).

What is the 1-D Diffusion Equation?
$\frac{\partial u}{\partial t} = K \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial x^2}$. It describes the movement of a quantity from high to low concentration.

What are OpenMP Tasks used for?
Irregular or unstructured parallelism (e.g., `while` loops, recursion) where the amount of work or the number of iterations is not known in advance.
