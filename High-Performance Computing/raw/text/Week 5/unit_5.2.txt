• There are five floating point exceptions specified by IEEE 754  
- Overflow: inf or -inf
- Underflow: 0 or subnormal number
- Divide by zero: inf or -inf
- Invalid: nan
- Inexact: mathematically inexact, returns rounded result 
• Floating point exceptions set a flags in the hardware and can be trapped for 
debugging
