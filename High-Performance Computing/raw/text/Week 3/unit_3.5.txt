Unit Summary
57
‚ÄĘ Finite differences can be used to calculate an approximation to a spatial derivative
‚ÄĘ We can use Taylor series to quantify the error in the approximation (the truncation 
error)
‚ÄĘ The size of the truncation error depends on the spacing between grid points (spatial 
resolution)
‚ÄĘ The leading term in the truncation error depends on the finite difference being used and 
affects the accuracy
