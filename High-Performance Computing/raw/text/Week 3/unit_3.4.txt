Unit Summary
47
‚ÄĘ The diffusion equation is a partial differential equation containing a time derivative and 
one or more second order spatial derivatives 
‚ÄĘ Second order spatial derivatives can also be calculated using a finite difference 
approximation 
‚ÄĘ We can calculate a numerical solution using time stepping and a finite difference stencil 
like the method used for the advection equation
