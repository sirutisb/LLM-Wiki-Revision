Point-to-point communication

--- page 2 ---
• With MPI (Message Passing Interface) processes communicate and co-ordinate by 
passing messages 
• One type of communication is point-to-point communication  
• Point-to-point communication takes place between a pair of processes in a 
communicator
• The most basic functions for sending and receiving point-to-point messages are 
MPI_Send and MPI_Recv
